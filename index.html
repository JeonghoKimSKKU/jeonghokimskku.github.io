김정호 / 金正浩 / Jeongho Kim

Political Science · Chinese Politics · Computational Social Science

I am a Research Assistant at the Sungkyun Institute of China Studies. I hold an M.A. in Political Science from Sungkyunkwan University and a B.A. in History from Fudan University.

I study elite politics, state–society relations, and authoritarian politics, with a focus on China. My research uses quantitative and computational methods, including text-as-data and large language models, to examine responsiveness, governance, and political communication under authoritarian regimes.
